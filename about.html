About This Tool

This application uses advanced deep learning to analyze dermatoscopic images and detect potential skin cancer lesions.

The AI model has been trained on thousands of medical-grade images to recognize patterns associated with different types of skin lesions. While this tool can provide valuable insights, it should be used as a supplementary tool and not as a replacement for professional medical advice.

AI Technology

This tool uses a state-of-the-art ResNet50 deep learning model that has been fine-tuned on dermatological images.

  • Advanced convolutional neural networks
  • Grad-CAM visualization for explainability
  • Feature-based analysis of lesion characteristics
  • Trained on the HAM10000 dataset of dermatoscopic images
Important Disclaimer

This tool is intended for educational purposes only.

The results provided by this application should not be used as a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of a qualified healthcare provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition.

If you have concerns about a skin lesion, please consult with a dermatologist or other healthcare professional.
